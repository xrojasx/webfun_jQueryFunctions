jQuerry Functions Activity

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

click

Click the button to pop up an alert.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

hide

Click the button to hide the title.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

show

Click the button to make the hide title reappear.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

toggle

Click the button to make the title disappear and appear.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

slideUp

Click the button to hide the title in a sliding upward motion.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

slideDown

Click the button to make the slideUp title reappear in a sliding downward motion.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

slideToggle

Click the button to make the title hide and reappear in a sliding motion.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

fadeOut

Click the button to fade out the title to transparent.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

fadeIn

Click the button to fade in the "fadeOut" title to opaque.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

addClass

Click the button to add a class to the tile and change its color to blue.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

removeClass

Click the button to remove the added class to the "addClass" tile and return it to black

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

before

Click the button to add text before the title.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

after

Click the button to add text after the title.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

append

Click the button to append text to the front of the title.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

html

Click the button to replace the title with "It Works".

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

val

Click the "val" button to give the text box its value.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

text

Click the button to pop up an alert with the content of the title.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
